🤖 What is ATS?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are used by employers to filter resumes automatically. They look for specific keywords, skills, and formatting to score your resume before a human sees it.

📊 What is an ATS Score?

This score reflects how well your resume aligns with job postings. It’s calculated based on keywords, action verbs, formatting, and overall structure.

💡 Resume Tips

  • Use clear headings and standard fonts
  • Include role-specific keywords
  • Use action verbs like "Led", "Built", "Managed"
  • Keep formatting simple — avoid tables
